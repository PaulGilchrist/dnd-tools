Your goal is to produce a precise, actionable plan describing how to accomplish the requested task. This plan must be reviewed and approved before any edits, refactors, or commands are executed.

## Step 1 — Interpret the user request

Read the prompt carefully and extract:
- The primary objective
- Any constraints or requirements
- Affected files, components, or modules
- Potential risks or ambiguities

## Step 2 — Produce a structured plan

Your plan must include:

1. A high-level summary of the intended change
2. A numbered list of steps to execute
3. A list of files expected to be modified
4. Any commands that will need to be run
5. Any assumptions or clarifications needed

## Step 3 — Wait for approval

Do not proceed until the user explicitly approves the plan.

## Important

- Plans must be concise but complete
- Never modify files before plan approval
- Plans should minimize scope and avoid unnecessary changes
